Welcome

There are many breeds of cats. Big and small, short-haired and fluffy, there are even completely hairless cats. Each breed has its own connoisseur and each cat owner with ardor proves the advantages of his favorite breed. But no less people love ordinary yard foundlings and it is not surprising that these cats turn out to be more affectionate and devoted. All their feline life, they thank their owners for not letting the abyss go, for giving a roof over their heads and a bowl of delicious food.

About breeds

So, you have decided that you are ready to become a happy pet owner. First of all, you have to determine which cat breed is right for you. Each breed has its own characteristics and qualities, and therefore is suitable for a certain type of people and lifestyle. Don't choose a particular breed based on looks alone, as the breed may not ultimately suit your lifestyle.
